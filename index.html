Monkey and Cloud

by Luann Suhr (Author), Nalla Tau (Illustrator)

The story of Monkey and Cloud is an exploration adventure through helping. Monkey helps the lost Cloud to find their family and has many wonderful experiences along the journey.

Throughout the story of Monkey and Cloud there are many things to learn (morals):

  1. To be grateful
  2. To be helpful to others
  3. To question things you might be afraid of
  4. To test things out and see (because maybe they are not actually so scary).
  5. To see the beauty in diversity.
  6. To enjoy their adventures.
  7. To remember their experiences and their friends.

Most of the characters are not a set gender so that anyone can imagine themselves as the main characters. (Only 2 characters have a set gender because they are who the book is dedicated to, the author's grandmother and niece).
